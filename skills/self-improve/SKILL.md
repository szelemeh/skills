# Self-improve

Reflect across the whole session for friction: where you stumbled, were corrected,
ground through trial and error, or misread the task from the outset. For each, trace
its root cause and name what was missing. Completion: every friction point, not the loudest few.

Then present a list of surgical changes to the setup. Draw on your self-knowledge
to find the cleanest home for each, preferring durable setup over memory:
an agent rule, a skill, the docs, AGENTS.md/CLAUDE.md, or wherever it best belongs.
